Our Services

Professional Landscaping

Burner Gardening Service offers comprehensive landscaping solutions for residential and commercial properties in West Valley City. Our experienced team specializes in landscape design, installation, and renovation, transforming outdoor spaces into functional, beautiful environments.

We work closely with clients to understand their vision, ensuring every project aligns with their aesthetic preferences and practical needs. From patios and walkways to water features and outdoor lighting, our services combine creativity with technical expertise.

Expert Garden Maintenance

Maintain your garden's health and beauty with our professional maintenance services. Our skilled gardeners provide regular care including pruning, weeding, fertilizing, and pest control.

We develop customized maintenance plans based on your garden's specific requirements and local climate conditions. Our team stays current with sustainable horticultural practices to ensure your plants thrive while minimizing environmental impact.

Sustainable Solutions

We prioritize eco-friendly practices in all our services, offering sustainable landscaping options that conserve resources and support local ecosystems. Our experts implement water-wise irrigation systems, drought-resistant native plants, and organic soil management techniques.

We specialize in xeriscaping designs that reduce water usage while maintaining visual appeal. Our sustainable approach includes using electric equipment, recycling green waste, and implementing natural pest control methods.

Seasonal Care Programs

Our seasonal care programs ensure your landscape remains healthy year-round in Utah's varying climate. Spring services include soil preparation, aeration, and planting, while summer focuses on irrigation optimization and pest management.

Fall cleanup involves leaf removal, perennial care, and winterization preparations. We also offer snow removal and winter protection services during colder months. Our team monitors local weather patterns to time interventions effectively.

Custom Landscape Design

Create your ideal outdoor space with our personalized design services. Our designers combine functionality with aesthetics to develop layouts that complement your property's architecture and natural features.

We consider factors like sun exposure, soil composition, and usage patterns to create practical, beautiful designs. Whether you want an entertainment-focused backyard or specialized features like edible gardens, we translate your ideas into actionable plans.
